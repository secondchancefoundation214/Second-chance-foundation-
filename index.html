How It Works

  1. Patients apply for support via our application form.
  2. We review and verify the eligibility and cost details.
  3. Approved patients pay 50% of the treatment cost up front.
  4. The Foundation directly pays the remaining 50% to the healthcare provider.
  5. Our donors make this possible—together, we give patients a second chance.
